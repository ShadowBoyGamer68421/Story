The great lord Gideon has died. The citizens were distraught with grief and sadness over his death. They gave him parting gifts such as flowers and prayed that his soul would rest in peace in the afterlife. The questiions on everyone's mind were "Who will usurp lord Gideon?" and "who is next in the line of succession?". The answer to these questions would be the lord's son Gideon II.
